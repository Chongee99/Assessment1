Learning Tests

I have always found these tests very reflective. Always nice to see how I learn, mostly I feel I know how I learn but I always tend to find out something new or different when I do these tests every year or so. I wasn’t surprised to see that I am still a pragmatic learner. I’ve always found that learning by doing or seeing practical ways things can be used make it easier for me to understand and take it all in. Normally I do find that I tend to take a leadership role when it comes to work and life, but will easily concede it to another if they want to try or if I find that they will be a better leader than me. Always want what I best for the group and task at hand.
